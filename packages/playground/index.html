Flooded World Component Playground

Isolated testing environment for flooded city building blocks — Year 40 post-flood

World Setting: Forty years after the waters rose, the city has "become its rooftops." Streets sit underwater at the 4th floor. Residents live on upper floors and rooftops connected by rope bridges, ferries, and salvaged infrastructure. Buildings rise from murky water, lantern light reflects on dark surfaces, and platform ferries glide between docking stations. Academy students patrol territories, diving for salvage in the flooded depths below.
