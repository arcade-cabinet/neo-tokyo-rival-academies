// NEO-TOKYO COMPONENT PLAYGROUND

Isolated testing environment for flooded city building blocks.

WORLD CONCEPT: Neo-Tokyo after global flooding. The city has "become its rooftops" - streets are underwater, residents live on upper floors and roofs connected by bridges, ferries, and ziplines. Buildings rise from murky water, neon reflects on dark surfaces, platform ferries glide on hidden rails.
