# Story Arcs & Timelines v1.0

**Overall Arc**: 3-hour playable narrative (Prologue → Act 1 → Act 2 → Act 3).
**Rivalry**: Vera acts as mirror; relationship evolves (Ally / Rival / Enemy) based on alignment.

## Act 1: Awakening (Prologue + ~45 min)
**Macro Beats**:
- **Prologue**: Player creation + arrival (fixed tutorial, no alignment yet).
- **Goal**: Complete Selection Ceremony → choose faction → first mission in Neon Slums → defeat Cyber-Yakuza Boss → unlock Mid-Stratum access.
- **Branch Hooks**: Early alignment choices set Kurenai/Azure lean.

**Meso Quest Clusters**:
1. **Arrival & Selection (Academy Gate Slums)**: Investigate holo-invite, drone patrol, rival intro. Outcome: Faction choice.
2. **Training Arc (Transition Fringe)**: Duel with mentor, stats tutorial, parkour course. Outcome: Faction lock-in.
3. **Neon Slums Mission (Neon Spire)**: Infiltrate HoloPlaza vault, defeat Yakuza Boss. Outcome: Data-core reward + Tournament invite.

## Act 2: Tournament of Rivals (~60 min)
**Macro Beats**:
- **Goal**: Compete in Tournament → face Vera → uncover conspiracy → choose deeper path.
- **Branch Hooks**: Alignment > |0.6| unlocks exclusive stratum access.

**Meso Quest Clusters**:
1. **Tournament Prep (Central Pillar)**: Register, scout opponents (hack/bribe), faction history.
2. **Skybridge Rounds (Rooftop Skybridge)**: Combat on elevated platforms, drone traffic hazards.
3. **Vera Showdown (Club Eclipse)**: Rivalry climax. Dialogue branch > 0.6 Azure (debate) vs < -0.6 Kurenai (duel).
4. **Conspiracy Fallout**: Faction enforcer or resistance traitor boss.

## Act 3: Mirror of Rivals (~60 min)
**Macro Beats**:
- **Goal**: Confront faction manipulation source → final showdown with Vera.
- **Branch Endings**: Azure Extreme, Kurenai Extreme, Neutral Mystery.

**Meso Quest Clusters**:
1. **Stratum Extremes Infiltration**: Corporate Pinnacle (Azure) or Fringe Resistance (Kurenai).
2. **Final Prep**: Mirror build preparation against Vera.
3. **Conspiracy Nexus Climax**: Final boss + Ending cutscene.

## Endings
- **Azure Victory**: Corporate truth, controlled future.
- **Kurenai Victory**: Passionate revolution, chaotic freedom.
- **Neutral Mystery**: Hidden third-path truth.
