# Story Arcs & Timelines

> **Purpose**: Define the three-act structure, quest clusters, and narrative progression.

## Overview

The game delivers **~3 hours of content** across three acts plus prologue/epilogue. The narrative weaves horizontal exploration with vertical progression, centered on the player's rivalry with Vera.

## Act Structure

```
Prologue (10 min) → Act 1 (30-45 min) → Act 2 (45-60 min) → Act 3 (30-45 min) → Epilogue (5 min)
```

**Total**: ~3 hours main path + ~1 hour side content

---

## Prologue: Arrival (10 min)

### Purpose
- Tutorial mechanics
- Establish Neo-Tokyo setting
- First impression of faction divide

### Beats
1. **Arrival Cutscene**: Player arrives at Academy Gate
2. **Basic Movement Tutorial**: Touch/keyboard controls
3. **First Combat**: Training dummy encounter
4. **Faction Introduction**: Observe Kurenai vs Azure students
5. **Transition**: Receive Selection Ceremony invitation

### Quest Cluster: None (Linear Tutorial)

---

|-----------------|--------|-----------------|

| -0.2 to +0.2 | True Neutral | Mystery revealed, third path |
| +0.2 to +0.6 | Azure Lean | Strategic partnership |
